Survey Methodology & Transparency

This section explains how the survey works, how information is protected, and how public results are calculated.

Data Collection

Responses are collected voluntarily through the survey form and stored securely in the database.

Public Results

Only combined statistics, percentages, charts, and participation counts are displayed publicly.

Privacy Protection

Names, email addresses, occupation, city, and personal comments are not displayed publicly.

Important Disclaimer

This platform is an independent public opinion survey. It is not affiliated with any government organisation, political party, election commission, ministry, or public authority. The results represent only the opinions of participants and are not official election results, government statistics, or scientific polling data.

Is this an official government survey?

No. This is an independent public opinion survey platform.

No. Personal information remains private and is not displayed publicly.


How are results calculated?

Results are calculated from submitted responses and displayed as aggregated numbers and percentages.


Can anyone participate?

Participation is voluntary and available to eligible users according to the survey rules.


Can I change my response later?

Responses are stored according to the platform's data policy.
