Mishu

There are names that feel like sound… and there are names that feel like emotion. Yours feels like something I never learned how to forget.

Some people enter life loudly. Some enter quietly and stay in every thought without permission. You were never loud… but you became permanent.

I don’t know when you became the background of my thoughts… but now even silence feels incomplete without you inside it.

There is a strange calm in the way I think about you… like everything unnecessary disappears when your name appears.

🌹🌸🌷

If beauty ever tried to describe itself… it would probably fail and just point towards you.

I never planned to feel this way… but feelings rarely ask for permission before becoming real.

Some emotions don’t need explanation. They just need honesty. And this is mine.

There are things I can say out loud… and there are things that only exist when I think of you.

This is one of them.

I don’t want to impress you. I just want to exist in a way that feels real to you.

If I ever look lost, it is only because I am thinking about you more than I should.

And now I don’t want to keep this inside anymore.

Not because I need an answer… but because you deserve to know what you became to me.

I don’t know how you will read this… but I hope you feel it more than you read it.

Will you marry me, Mishu?
