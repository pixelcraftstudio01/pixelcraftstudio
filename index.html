Teen Mass & Muscle Gain Plan

Daily Diet Plan

  • Morning: Warm water + banana/apple + optional milk with cocoa
  • Breakfast: 3 eggs scrambled OR paneer bhurji OR 1 cup curd; 2 rotis OR oats porridge with honey & fruits; peanut butter optional
  • Mid-Morning Snack: Handful of roasted peanuts/almonds/walnuts; 1 fruit (mango, guava, papaya, apple)
  • Lunch: 2-3 rotis OR 1 cup rice; 1 cup dal OR rajma/chole; 1 cup mixed vegetables; salad with cucumber, tomato, lemon
  • Evening Snack / Pre-Workout: Sprouts salad OR boiled chickpeas with spices; 1 glass milk or fruit shake
  • Post-Workout: Milk + banana OR curd + oats; optional whey protein (if approved)
  • Dinner: 2-3 rotis OR small rice portion; 1 cup dal OR eggs OR paneer; 1 cup vegetables; salad optional
  • Before Bed: 1 glass milk OR 2-3 soaked almonds

Tips & Notes

  • Focus on a balanced diet first; supplements only fill gaps
  • Drink 2–3 liters of water daily
  • Sleep 7–9 hours for recovery and growth
  • Moderate peanut butter, honey, and cocoa for extra calories
  • Consistency and patience are key for healthy muscle gain

*Always check with a parent/guardian and doctor before starting supplements.*
